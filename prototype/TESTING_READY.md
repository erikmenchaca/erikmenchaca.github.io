**Introduction (5 min)**

- Get consent
- Baseline questions

- Show interface
- Set scenario (Alex Chen, UX Designer)

- Open-ended questions
- Overall impressions

**Questionnaires (5-7 min)**
- SUS (10 questions)
- Custom research questions (12 questions)

**Wrap-up (2 min)**
- Thank participant
- Compensate
- Stop recording
